Geometric Fox

Beginner

A simple yet elegant fox design with clean geometric lines. Perfect for beginners and a great decoration for any desk or shelf.

Dimensions

15 x 10 x 12 cm

Assembly Time

15 minutes

Paper Type

160-200 gsm

Pages

2 sheets

Assembly Instructions

  1. 1

    Print the template

    Print the template on cardstock paper (160-200 gsm). Regular printer paper will work but the model will be less sturdy.

  2. 2

    Cut along solid lines

    Using scissors or a craft knife, carefully cut along all solid lines of the template.

  3. 3

    Score along dotted lines

    Using a ruler and the back of a craft knife (or a scoring tool), score along all dotted lines to make folding easier and cleaner.

  4. 4

    Fold along scored lines

    Fold all scored lines, making sure to create sharp creases. Mountain folds go up, valley folds go down.

  5. 5

    Apply glue to tabs

    Apply a small amount of glue to each tab. We recommend using a glue stick or craft glue.

  6. 6

    Assemble the model

    Start by attaching the head pieces, then work your way down to the body. Hold each piece in place for a few seconds until the glue sets.
